## In the process of designing a Breathwork App.
## The app focuses on 3 main divisions. **Align. Activate. Aim**

# Align 
## Align your breath and body to release tension and settle into calm, restoring balance and relaxation.

# Activate
## Activate your inner power with energizing breaths that awaken vitality and lift your state naturally.

# Aim
## Aim your attention through focused breathing to steady the mind and sharpen concentration.
