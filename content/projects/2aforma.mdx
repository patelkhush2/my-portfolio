# Overview
## Forma is a structure-first writing environment that rethinks how AI supports thinking.

## Instead of replying in chat bubbles, Forma transforms messy drafts into structured arguments – building clarity and logical gaps. AI operates as a structural lens rather than a content machine.

## Forma explores a simple idea: productivity improves when ambiguity is reduced. By focusing on hierarchy, clarity, and responsible AI feedback, the product treats structure as the core unit of thinking.
